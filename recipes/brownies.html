Description

These brownies are not for the faint of heart! If you are a chocolate addict these are definetly for you!

Ingredients

  • 220g Butter
  • 250 Chocolate
  • 320g Sugar
  • 4 Eggs
  • 120g Flour
  • 50g Cacao Powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C
  2. Melt the butter and gently stir in the chocolate until combined
  3. Take it of the stove and add the sugar and the eggs. Mix until combined
  4. In a larger bowl combine flour and sifted cocoa poweder.
  5. Add all ingredients together and stir until smooth
  6. Pour the mixture in the sheeted form and bake at 160°C for around 40 minutes
  7. Remove the brownies and let them cool before serving
