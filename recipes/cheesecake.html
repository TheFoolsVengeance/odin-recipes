Description

The following recipes is for a simple yet deliciously rich New York style cheesecake. It is perfect for beginners dipping their toes into the wonderful world of baking and more seasoned veterans of the craft alike.

The base will be made out of crushed (or rather pulverited) biscuits and some molten butter. On top of will be the heart of the cheesecake, consisting of just four ingredients: Lavish amounts of cream cheese, sugar, a bit of sour cream and three whole eggs.

But don't be fooled: Together those following few ingredients combine to make something that packs serious punch, while at the same time having a velvety smooth feel to it!

Ingredients

  • 150g Butter
  • 250g Biscuits
  • 795g Cream Cheese
  • 250g Sour Cream
  • 150g Sugar
  • 3 Eggs

Steps

  1. Melt the butter on the stove and crush (or blend) the biscuits
  2. Take the pot of molten butter of the stove and mix in the crushed biscuits
  3. Combine and spread out the mixture over the bottom of a sheeted spring form
  4. In a seperate bowl, combine cream cheese, sour cream and sugar. Add eggs individually.
  5. Use some additional butter to grease the insides of the spring form
  6. Gently pour in the mixture and distribute evenly
  7. Bake at 180Â°C for 50 to 55 minutes
  8. Let it rest in the form until cool before cutting
