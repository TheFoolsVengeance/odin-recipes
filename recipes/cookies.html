Chocolate Chip Cookies

Home

Description

Ingredients

  • 220g Butter
  • 180g Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 360g Flour
  • 120g Chocolate

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C
  2. In a large bowl, combine warm butter with sugar
  3. Add egg and flour seperately to the mixture
  4. Mix hacked chocolate into the mixture until evently distributed
  5. Form about 8 to 12 balls out of the dough by hand and place them on a sheeted baking tray
  6. Bake by 180°C for 10 to 12 minutes
